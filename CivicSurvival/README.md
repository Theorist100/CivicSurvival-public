# Civic Survival

**Infrastructure Crisis Simulator for Cities: Skylines II**

Hardcore energy management meets social satire. Control the grid, profit from chaos, survive the consequences.

## Concept

Your city's power grid is now your responsibility—and your opportunity. Manage blackouts strategically, sell energy to neighbors while your citizens freeze, and watch society react to your decisions.

## Features

### Situation Room
Advanced command dashboard with three tactical tabs:
- **Operations** — Real-time grid monitoring, district control, load shedding
- **Schemes** — Shadow energy exports, corruption mechanics, regulatory override
- **Resilience** — Backup systems, threat assessment, survival preparation

### Manual Load Shedding
- Toggle power per district and category (Residential, Commercial, Industrial, Office)
- Set priorities: who loses power first when demand exceeds supply
- Schedule presets: Manual, 4on/4off, 2on/4off, Day Only

### Corruption System
- Export surplus energy for offshore profit
- Attract public attention with suspicious activities
- Face investigations, protests, and legal consequences

### Social Impact
- Citizens react to blackouts with protests
- Neighbors envy those who still have power
- Media coverage of your "infrastructure decisions"

## Controls

Click the **PWR** button in the top-right corner to open the Situation Room dashboard.

## Requirements

- Cities: Skylines II
- **[Code Mod Tools](https://mods.paradoxplaza.com/mods/74417/Windows)** (required dependency)

## Installation

1. Extract to your Mods folder:
   `%LOCALAPPDATA%Low\Colossal Order\Cities Skylines II\Mods\CivicSurvival\`
2. Enable the mod in-game
3. Click the PWR button to access the dashboard
